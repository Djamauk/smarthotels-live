The Smart Room System

A plug-and-play renovation system that turns complex, expensive hotel room builds into a precise, repeatable process — the same way a car is assembled on a production line.

The One-Key Solution

Three modular categories

Every Smart Hotel room is built on three integrated component categories. Together they reduce a full room's smart electrical installation to just 60 minutes.

Category I

Lighting

  • 9W / 24° honeycomb grid spotlights
  • 8W high-efficiency flexible LED strips
  • High-efficiency reading lights
  • Ambient scenes: Work, Sleep, Welcome
  • Smart dimming with centralized control
  • 27,050 kWh saved annually per 100 rooms
Category II

Electrical & Control

  • Small black box universal ECU controllers
  • 6-in-1 integrated control panels
  • Voice, telephony, gateway, AC, lighting & switches in one
  • Matte brown power outlets
  • USB-C and Type-A charging sockets
  • Single interface for the entire room
Category III

Wire Harness

  • Pre-fabricated TV wall panel wiring
  • Interrupted power connectors
  • Uninterruptible power connectors
  • Three-pin socket extension cords
  • Fully plug-and-play — no custom wiring
  • 60-minute full-room installation time

Speed & Simplicity

A room in 60 minutes

Traditional hotel room wiring is slow, labour-intensive, and error-prone. The Smart Hotel system replaces it entirely with pre-fabricated wire harnesses and plug-and-play components — the same manufacturing logic used in automotive production.

TV screens, bed panels, and mirrors all snap into place. There is no need for specialist electricians on-site for extended periods. A local contractor with our standard operating procedures can complete a full room installation in under 60 minutes.

At scale, this means a 100-room hotel renovation is completed in a fraction of the time — minimising the revenue impact of rooms being offline and getting your property back to full operation faster.
