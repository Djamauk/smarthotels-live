The Case for Smart

Why UK hotel owners are paying attention

01

Drastic Cost Reduction

Our plug-and-play solutions replace complex traditional wiring and RCUs with integrated Smart Control Boxes and pre-assembled wire harnesses. A 100-room renovation saves approximately £123,300 over five years, with labour that previously took days now completed in under 60 minutes per room.

02

Minimal Disruption

The modular approach — covering flooring, wallpaper, smart bathroom units, and lighting — enables a "one-click" renovation. Construction time is shortened by over 30 days, construction waste is reduced by 80%, and rooms return to service faster.

03

Intelligent Guest Experience

6-in-1 integrated control panels manage voice, telephony, AC, lighting, and curtains from a single interface. Ambient lighting scenes (Work, Sleep, Welcome) deliver high emotional value. Smart lighting in corridors and public areas saves 27,050 kWh annually per 100 rooms — approximately £3,500/year.

04

Long-Term Reliability

Where the industry standard is a 1-year warranty, the Smart Hotel programme offers a 3-year automotive-grade warranty on all components. Maintenance risk is reduced to near zero, with full consulting, implementation, and after-care support included.
