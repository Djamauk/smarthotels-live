The Group Behind the Programme

Backed by a Global Leader

The Smart Hotel UK Programme is built on the foundation of H World Group — one of the world's top four hotel operators, with over 12,700 hotels, 31 brands, and nearly 280 million loyalty members.

H World Group

One of the world's fastest-growing hotel groups

Founded in 2005 and headquartered in Shanghai, H World Group (NASDAQ: HTHT | HKEX: 01179) has grown into a globally leading hotel operation managing over 12,702 hotels and 1.2 million rooms across 31 brands.

In July 2025, HOTELS Magazine ranked H World Group fourth globally. On its 'Global Top 50 Hotel Brands' list, Hanting Hotel ranked first and Ji Hotel ranked fourth — both brands powered by the same technology now being introduced to the UK market.

In 2020, H World completed the 100% acquisition of Deutsche Hospitality (rebranded H World International in 2024), signalling an active international expansion strategy. This UK programme represents the next step in that global growth.

The UK Programme

Smart Hotel Group in the UK

The Smart Hotel Group of Companies is the UK vehicle for bringing H World's proven technology programme to independent hotel owners, developers, and investors. We are represented by Autotec and Richard Lin, the architect of the One-Key modular system used across H World's portfolio.

Our role in the UK is not just to supply components — it is to act as a strategic partner. We provide feasibility and cost-benefit analysis, design and tech integration, supply chain management, and implementation oversight, ensuring local contractors can execute the rapid-assembly model to the same standard achieved across 12,000 hotels in Asia.

We are currently focussed on London, Manchester, Birmingham, Edinburgh, and Leeds — targeting independent hotels in the economy and mid-scale segments where the return on investment is most compelling.
